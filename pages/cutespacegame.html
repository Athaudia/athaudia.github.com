Athaudia's Cute Space Game

Athaudia's Cute Space Game is a free top down 360-degrees retro shooter for PC. Fight against waves of AI controlled ships, earn money, buy upgrades(15 weapons, 1 util and 2 different ships, with many more coming) and fight against more ships to reach a new high score.

It is currently in development, written in ruby, and either available as source download or a .exe for Windows.
